Usage:

Introduction
This is a demonstration showing how Alice goes to Dave's coffee shop and buys coffee (200 MELON points). Alice doesn't actually have the MELON points, so instead she buys the coffee by converting AIRSKY points first.

Step 1. Order
  • Open Dave's Order page

  • Click the Order button
  • A QR code appears
  • Click the QR code. The content is copied into the clipboard.

Step 2. Payment
  • Open Alice's Wallet page

  • Paste the previously copied content into the ADDRESS
  • Click outside the input field to reveal the product information
  • Scroll down to see the list of points. AIRSKY is the only one you can click, because it's the only one with enough balance.
  • A Confirmation dialog appears. Click Yes.

Step 3. Confirmation
  • Open Dave's Order List page

  • The top item in the list will have changed to Paid
